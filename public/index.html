Information


  • Instructions: Click anywhere within the test and begin typing to measure your typing speed. To restart the test at anytime, click the "Restart" button.
  • Language: this drop down changes the langauge of the test.
  • Restart: This button resets the timer and provides a new set of words for the next test
  • Time Left: Timer that indicates how much time is left in the test in secods
  • Mistakes: Each time a mistake is made, this number will increase by 1
  • WPM: Words Per Minute (WPM) is the most common measurement of typing speed and indicates how many words can be typed in 1 minute (60 seconds)
  • CPM: Characters Per Minute (CPM) is the measurement that indicatesthe amount of individual characters can be typed in 1 minute (60 seconds)
